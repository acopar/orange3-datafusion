IMDb Actors
===========

![IMDB Actors widget icon](icons/geo-data-sets.png)

Constructs a movie-actor and actor-actor relation matrix.

Signals
-------

**Inputs**:

- **Filter**

  Data filter.

**Outputs**:

- **Movie Actors**

  A movie-actor relation matrix.

- **Costarring Actors**

  An actor-actor relation matrix.
