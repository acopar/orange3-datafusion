Mean Fuser
==========

![Mean Fuser widget icon](icons/mean-fuser.png)

Constructs a mean fusion graph.

Signals
-------

**Inputs**:

- **Fusion Graph**

  Collective matrix.
  
- **Relation**

  Relations between two groups of objects.

**Outputs**:

- **Mean-fitted fusion graph**

  Mean-fitted collective matrix.

- **Relation**

  Relations between two groups of objects.
