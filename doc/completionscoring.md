Completion Scoring
==================

![Completion Scoring widget icon](icons/completion-scoring.png)

Scores the quality of matrix completion using root mean squared error (RSME).

Signals
-------

**Inputs**:

- **Fitted fusion graph**

  Fitted collective matrix.

- **Relation**

  Relations between two groups of objects.

**Outputs**:

- (None)
